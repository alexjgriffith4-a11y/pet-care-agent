For a healthy adult golden retriever like Buddy, a good baseline is to bathe him about once every 3 months, with more frequent baths only if he gets especially dirty, has been swimming, or has a skin issue that your veterinarian wants you to manage more closely.

Golden retrievers have a dense double coat, so over-bathing can dry the skin and strip natural oils from the coat. Regular brushing is usually more important than frequent bathing for keeping the coat healthy and managing shedding.

Use a dog-specific shampoo rather than human products, and adjust the schedule based on Buddy's activity level, coat condition, and any veterinary guidance. [Source 1]
